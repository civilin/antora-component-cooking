Brown rice is a set-it-and-forget-it item. Cook this first while you are preparing another dish.

. Put 2 cups brown rice in pressure cooker.
. Add 2 1/2 to 3 cups water (or broth).
. Pressure cook on High Pressure for 22 minutes.
