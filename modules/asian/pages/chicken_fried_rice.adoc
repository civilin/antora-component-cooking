= Chicken Fried Rice

Easy recipe for making tasty chicken fried rice in your pressure cooker.

== Ingredients

====
1-2 cups strips of pre-cooked grilled chicken into bite-sized pieces. +
8 oz can water chestnuts, rinsed and drained +
1 tbsp butter (or oil) +
1 medium onion, diced +
2 cloves garlic, minced +
1 egg +
1 cup basmati rice (uncooked) +
 1/4 cup soy sauce +
1 1/2 cup chicken stock +
1/2 cup frozen peas or other preferred vegetable +
====

== Instructions

. Heat the pressure cooker on sauté setting.
. Place the oil in the pot. Add the onion and garlic. Sauté 1 minute.
. Add the egg and scramble it with the rest of the ingredients for 1-2 minutes.
. Add the rice, soy sauce and chicken stock and stir well.
. Pressure cook for 12 minutes.
. Remove the lid and stir in frozen peas or veggies.
